# The map

KunstmeileMap shows all tents of an event on an interactive map. This page
explains the basics.

![Map overview with tent markers](/assets/screenshots/user/map-overview.png)

## Pan & zoom

- **Pan:** drag with the mouse (desktop) or swipe with your finger (mobile).
- **Zoom:** mouse wheel or the +/− buttons (desktop) or pinch with two fingers
  (mobile).

## Understanding markers

Each tent is a **marker** on the map. The color comes from the tent's first
category.

- At low zoom, markers are shown as compact dots.
- As you zoom in, the **tent numbers** appear as badges:

![Numbered markers at high zoom](/assets/screenshots/user/marker-detail.png)

For food, drink or service tents an **icon** is shown instead of the number
(e.g. 🍔, 🍺, ☕, 🅿️) – so they're recognizable at a glance.

A **click/tap** on a marker opens the tent's detail view (see
[Selecting a tent](/en/user/select-tent)).

## Switch the map style

Use the style toggle to switch between **street map** and **satellite imagery**:

![Satellite view](/assets/screenshots/user/map-satellite.png)

The choice is stored in the browser and kept for your next visit.

## Switch language

At the top you can switch between **German** and **English**. Descriptions,
categories and the interface adapt immediately.
