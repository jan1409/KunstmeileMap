# Using it on mobile

KunstmeileMap is optimized for mobile devices. This page summarizes the
specifics of mobile use.

![Map on a smartphone](/assets/screenshots/user/mobile-map.png)

## Operating the map

- **Pan:** swipe with your finger.
- **Zoom:** pinch with two fingers.
- **Open a tent:** tap a marker.

## Expand/collapse the menu

On small screens, search and categories are combined behind the **menu icon**
(☰). Tapping it expands them:

![Mobile menu (search & categories)](/assets/screenshots/user/mobile-menu.png)

## Detail drawer

When you tap a tent, the detail view opens as a **drawer from the bottom**
(bottom sheet):

![Detail drawer on mobile](/assets/screenshots/user/mobile-drawer.png)

- Drag the **handle** upward to see more.
- Swipe down to close the drawer.

## Viewing photos

Photos open full screen. Swipe sideways to **browse**, tap the background to
**close**.

## Take a photo (for authorized users)

Signed-in contributors see an **Add photo** button in the tent view that opens
the **camera** directly – ideal for taking pictures on site.
